Our Mission

Financial infrastructure
should be public.

Traditional crowdfunding platforms take 5–8% in fees, require identity verification, and restrict access by geography or banking status. ChainFund eliminates all of that.

By placing campaign logic entirely on the Ethereum blockchain, creators receive exactly what backers pledge — deadline rules enforced automatically, every transaction permanently auditable by anyone, anywhere, forever.

Our Values

What we stand for

Every decision we make is guided by four principles that define how we build, ship, and support the ChainFund ecosystem.

01
🔓

Openness

Our smart contract is fully open-source and verified on Etherscan. Every line of code is readable by anyone. No black boxes, no hidden logic — ever.

02
⚖️

Fairness

Zero platform fees on contributions. Every wei of ETH goes directly toward the campaign goal. We sustain ourselves through optional CTK mechanics, not by skimming backer funds.

03
🌍

Inclusivity

If you have an Ethereum wallet, you can participate — regardless of where you live, what bank account you hold, or what government-issued ID you possess.

04
🛡️

Trust

Smart contract rules are immutable post-deploy. No admin keys exist to change campaign terms. No rug pulls, no silent upgrades — what you see is what you get.
