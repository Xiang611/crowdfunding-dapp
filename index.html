🌿 Open Source · Community Driven

Building the future of
decentralised giving

ChainFund was born out of a belief that fundraising should be open, borderless, and free from gatekeeping — powered by code, not intermediaries.

Our Mission

We believe financial infrastructure should be public. Traditional crowdfunding platforms take 5–8% fees, require identity verification, and restrict access by geography or banking status. ChainFund eliminates all of that.

By placing campaign logic entirely on the Ethereum blockchain, we ensure that creators receive exactly what backers pledge, deadline rules are enforced automatically, and every transaction is permanently auditable by anyone, anywhere, forever.
